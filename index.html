    No one really knows how they found each other, but it came as no surprise when two substance addicts got hitched in the heat of bliss. People didn’t really care to take notice of the pair until the woman turned up on a slinger’s porch with a belly bloated in pregnancy. She gave birth to a baby boy and gave him the name “Victor,” after her own father (deceased). Victor learned to fend for himself from a very early age, but that wasn’t anything abnormal in the Slums. Between his physically abusive alcoholic father, and mentally absent narcotic addicted mother, it was pretty much impossible for him to find some reliable parental support. He inherited the entirety of his looks from mother dearest in a literal and metaphorical sense, and quickly gained a reputation for being an adorable child. At the time, Victor would say he was mistaken for a little girl at least twice a day. He never bothered to correct anyone, it just felt right … and it earned him a great deal of favor as a beggar.

    As time passed he got better at the game of survival and earned a bit of a reputation, just enough to cement himself the nickname: “Vee.” The more he grew the more he embraced androgyny, which was good and all, but it attracted some unfortunate attention. Unbeknownst to the youth, his mother owed one of the big gangs a depressingly massive debt, and while they debated on how they’d collect from her they caught wind of her kid. Vee’s was taken as payment and--unfortunately--sold off to human traffickers for a fat stack.

    The details get a bit fuzzy after that. Vee remembers barely surviving the discovery that he didn’t have girl parts, his homosexuality ended up saving his life. Apparently there was a really expensive market for gay afeminite men. Prostitution became his life. This period of time was really brutal on him, criminals and bottom-of-the-barrel slum folk didn’t make for gentle respectable clients. Every instance of violent mistreatment only served to make Vee’s stubborn fighting spirit angrier, and angrier, until one day he snapped. He murdered a customer and escaped.

    It was only thanks to the benevolence of a local preacher that he survived the ordeal. Father Millwire kept the young escapee safe in a secret sanctuary until the dead man’s connections stopped hunting for his killer. This provided Vee a lot of time to self reflect, process all the trauma, and metamorphosize. He emerged as a She, confident in her newfound identity. Victor changed her name to Veronica and never looked back. She ended up finding work in a local (strip) Club; why look for a new job opportunity? Using her body for money was all she knew at this point. Years later she came across a young boy running through the streets (with his woes) and he seemed to change her luck for the better. His dad, the fucking mayor, has hired her into what he calls the “Task Force.” Veronica, of all people. She’s down, she can dig it. New life, new me.

    Vee's Talents

    Remorseless
    Vee can completely disassociate when engaging in combat, entering a point of terrifying mental stability that blocks out all of her emotions. Focusing on a single goal allows her to tunnel vision through what has to be done in order to achieve it. When she is fighting strangers or unfamiliar beings she gains a flat +5 to Strength. While fighting people and beings she already knows (or knows of) she gains +5 in any stat of choice for each rotation of combat, this caps at 5 rotations.

    Persuasive
    A powerful mother-hen instinct makes her a very relatable person that can give off “safe” and “trustworthy” vibes. While interacting with an individual Vee can access a talent that creates a unique stat known as “Charisma,” which adds her Intellect and Dexterity values together. Charisma is useful for getting people to open up to her, or unnaturally impart the idea that she is their best friend regardless of their true relationship. Those who don’t have a high enough Intelligence stat to resist her charms (within reason) will be effortlessly manipulated into a sense of security.

    Graceful
    When engaging in activities influenced heavily by exercise (mainly for combat but not exclusively) Vee is able to tap into a unique stat known as “Finesse,” determined by the sum of her Speed and Dexterity stats. Finesse is heavily influenced by her history in acrobatic dancing, allowing for fully fluid--and practiced--athletic movement with a heavy dose of increased flexibility. While this talent is in use her Stamina will gradually decrease over time (-1 stat per rotation after being used up to 3 rotations) and rest outside of combat will be required to fully restore it.

    Vee's Skills

    Gaslight
    In the event that Vee fails to be Persuasive enough with another individual (regardless if they are player or npc) she gets really angry, and gets a +5 to strength until the anger subsides.

    Precision Strike
    Vee has lots of practice with hand-to-hand combat, and can apply this training in a special way. She can make small heavy-hitting attacks in precise motions when in close range with an opponent, swapping her Speed and Strength values for the very short duration of time that a single strike takes.

    Steady Hand
    Fluid motion isn’t just good for dancing, it also becomes quite useful in application of knowledge regarding human biology. With extensive study comes a distinct ability to almost surgically target the natural weak points any average human would possess (the throat, stomach, heart, etc). While actively aiming to target soft spots, Vee gains a flat +10 to her Dexterity.

    Active Buffs

  • Every twenty points in Intellect, you get an additional stat point plus all the ones, you missed previously. Only four of these points may be placed into intellect.

  • Speed I → Passage of Time; This is a passive trigger based skill, which is awakened in characters who hold twenty speed or more. In critical moments, which death is highly possibly, they will burn stamina and enter a state where time would have seemed to have slowed (15%). In reality, they have began to move faster than before. Some say they even seen vectors in this state as they master it. (+7 speed)

  • Dex 1 → Ashley's Wardrobe: When crafting or doing any endeavor that requires a slight of hand, you may take your next highest stat (The next highest stat besides dex, so if dex is your second highest. It will go to your third highest stat.)and combine it into your dex. This increases your chances of creating higher quality goods from low quality items and may auto-success a difficult check in some situations.

    • Acquired Innovations

    Deep Meditation
    Upon entering a deep meditative state of being, this technique will allow an individual to re-evaluate themselves with thorough self reflection. It allows them to temporarily change their stats around however they desire, assuming they are able to maintain an uninterrupted peaceful state for three hours. If the process is interrupted the user will suffer a debuff to Dexterity (-2) and Intellect (-1). Stat changes remain for the remainder of that day (12 hours). Deep Meditation can be taught by Veronica to other people, but they have to have at least 10 Intellect and 10 Dexterity in order to learn/reciprocate the ability.

    Shocking Relic
    A machete-sized blade with a slight curve to its upper end that almost resembles a Persian “shamshir” in appearance, but is far more sleek and modern. Almost futuristic in its aesthetic, the smooth steel-esque metal is confined neatly into a leather-bound hilt. There’s a strange flat black pad that could be made from some form of super advanced plastic on the butt of the hilt which functions as a solar power source. Aside from being made of very lightweight and durable materials, the blade emits an electrical shock as potent as a taser: enough to lock up someone’s muscles and stun them into a temporary immovable state for a single post after successful application of the shock to their system. The shock feature can be used up to 10 times before a period of recharge is required to regain all charges.
